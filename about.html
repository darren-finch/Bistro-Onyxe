Proud Italian entrees, humble beginnings.

We’ve come a long way over the past 50 years. Here’s a little snapshot into our journey - and the journey of our master chef Alessandro Romano.

See Our Progress

1958

Alessandro grew up in a poor family, but that didn't stop him. At age 8, he cooks his first dish of pasta for his sick mother - and doesn't look back.

After many dishes of pasta...

1971

Alessandro inherits a street corner museum that had closed down. He seizes the opportunity, and Bistro Onyxe was born.

Once he was sick of fettucine alfredo...

1990

Alessandro Romano partners with his lifelong friend & side-cook Roberto Rissi. Together, they triple Bistro Onyxe’s popularity.

Many glasses of wine later...

2021

After 50 years of serving exquisite Italian culinary experiences to Manhattan, all we can say is that we are blessed.
